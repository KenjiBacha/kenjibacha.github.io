GLaDOS


Portal 1

GLaDOS (aka the Gentic Lifeform and Disk Operating System) is the main villian of the first game of the Portal franchise. She is acts as a proctor for the various test chambers the player solves, making snide remarks occasionally. Once the player finally meets GLaDOS face to face, she gloats about how she killed all of the human scientist in the facility, and attempt to kill the player as well.

Portal 2

In the second game, GLaDOS resumes her role as test chamber proctor with a degree of extra snark as she constantly reminds the player that they murdered her. However, at the half way point, GLaDOS is forced out of Apetures mainframe, having to survive of the energy provided by a potato battery.
